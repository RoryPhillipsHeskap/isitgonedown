Why is YouTube not working?

1. A Google-side outage

YouTube is a Google service and its outages are rare but high-impact when they happen. If YouTube, Gmail and Google Search all fail at once, it's almost certainly a Google infrastructure issue.

2. Your internet speed

Video streaming needs consistent bandwidth. Slow or variable connections cause the dreaded "spinning circle" mid-playback. Test your speed at fast.com — under 3 Mbps makes HD unreliable.

3. Ad blocker conflict

YouTube has aggressively cracked down on ad blockers. Videos may refuse to play or pause repeatedly if a blocker is detected. Try disabling your blocker on youtube.com.

4. Browser cache or cookies

Corrupted YouTube cookies cause endless "sign in to continue" loops or a video that loads but refuses to play. Clear cookies for youtube.com specifically.

5. Region restriction on the video

If YouTube works but a specific video says "Not available in your country", that video is region-locked by the uploader or by copyright. A VPN to a different country typically unlocks it.

6. Outdated browser or app

Very old browsers stop getting YouTube updates. If YouTube suddenly won't load at all, try a different browser or update your current one.

Is YouTube down for everyone or just me?

Google has extremely resilient infrastructure, so a full YouTube outage is rare. Most of the time when YouTube "isn't working", the problem is on the user's side — connection, cache, or ad blocker.

What to do if YouTube is only down for you

  • Try Incognito / Private mode. Bypasses cached cookies and extensions. If it works there, clear your browser cache.
  • Disable ad blockers. YouTube actively breaks videos when it detects blocking.
  • Try a different browser. Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari.
  • Switch DNS. Change to 1.1.1.1 or 8.8.8.8 if YouTube won't resolve at all.
  • Log out of YouTube. A stale login token can silently break playback.
  • Check on mobile. If the mobile app works but desktop doesn't, it's your computer or network.

Frequently asked questions

Is YouTube down right now?

Run a live check above to see global status and real-time community reports.

Why are YouTube videos not loading?

Usually either an ad blocker conflict, bandwidth, or cached cookies — not a YouTube outage. Google's infrastructure is very resilient.

Is YouTube TV down?

YouTube TV can be down independently of regular YouTube because it depends on live-streaming infrastructure. Check specifically.

Is YouTube Music down?

Music shares YouTube's backend. If YouTube is up but Music isn't playing, try logging out or switching app / web.

Why is one video unavailable?

That single video is region-locked, age-restricted, removed, or copyright-blocked — not a YouTube outage.
