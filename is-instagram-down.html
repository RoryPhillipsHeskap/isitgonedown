Why is Instagram not working?

1. A Meta-side outage

Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp and Threads all share Meta infrastructure. When Meta has issues, Instagram is usually affected alongside Facebook and Messenger. Community reports spike simultaneously across all four.

2. Feed cache and old sessions

Instagram aggressively caches your feed locally. If the app shows stale content or fails to refresh, force-closing and reopening often fixes it before any server-side issue.

3. App version out of date

Instagram pushes server changes that break old app versions. If everyone else's Instagram is fine but yours keeps erroring, check the App Store or Play Store for an update.

4. ISP or country-level block

Instagram is restricted or throttled in some regions and many corporate or school networks block social media. If Instagram is unreachable only on one network, this is usually the cause.

5. Account locked or shadow-banned

If Instagram is up for everyone but you're stuck at a login error or your content doesn't appear in feeds, the issue is account-specific. Check your notifications and the Instagram Help Center.

6. Reels or DM backend issue

Reels and direct messages run on separate services. It is common for one to go down while the main feed stays up. Community reports often distinguish "Reels not loading" from a full Instagram outage.

Is Instagram down for everyone or just me?

This is the key question. If Instagram is down globally, there's nothing to do but wait — Meta outages usually resolve in under an hour. If Instagram is only down for you, a quick fix is almost always possible.

What to do if Instagram is only down for you

  • Force close and reopen the app. Swipe it away and reopen — clears most transient issues.
  • Try Instagram Web. Go to instagram.com in a browser. If that works, it's an app problem, not a network problem.
  • Log out and back in. Refreshes your session token and fixes many "content unavailable" errors.
  • Update the app. Check the App Store or Play Store.
  • Switch networks. Toggle Wi-Fi off to use mobile data. If Instagram works there, your Wi-Fi network is the issue.
  • Clear app cache. On Android: Settings → Apps → Instagram → Storage → Clear cache.

Frequently asked questions

Is Instagram down right now?

Run an instant check with the button above. You'll see global status plus real-time community reports from other users.

Is Instagram DM down but the feed works?

DMs run on separate infrastructure and can fail independently. If the global check shows Instagram is up but your DMs are broken, it is usually a partial outage.

Why can't I load Reels?

Reels need heavier video infrastructure and often break first. Try switching networks, clearing cache, or reinstalling the app.

Does a VPN help if Instagram is blocked?

Yes. If Instagram is blocked by your country, ISP, school or workplace, a VPN routes traffic through a different network. A VPN won't help if Instagram itself is down.
