Why is Snapchat not working?

1. A Snapchat-side outage

Snapchat has regular brief outages. When Snapchat itself is down, Snaps won't send, Stories won't load, and the app often just shows a loading spinner. Community reports spike fast.

2. Snaps stuck at "sending"

A Snap stuck at "sending" often means your phone lost signal mid-send. The Snap is queued locally and will send automatically when your connection returns.

3. Corrupt app cache

Snapchat caches heavily. A corrupted cache can make the app crash, show the wrong chat, or get stuck loading. Clear cache from within the Snapchat app under Settings.

4. Account lockout

Snapchat bans third-party app tweaks and jailbroken devices. If you're locked out, it's an account issue, not an outage.

5. Outdated app version

Snapchat retires old versions. If the app suddenly won't open and everyone else is fine, check for an update.

6. Network issue

Some workplace and school networks block Snapchat. If Snapchat fails on Wi-Fi but works on mobile data, your network is filtering it.

Is Snapchat down for everyone or just me?

Snapchat outages tend to be brief — usually 10-30 minutes. If Snaps are stuck at "sending" but your phone is on Wi-Fi, the issue is almost always Snapchat-side.

What to do if Snapchat is only down for you

  • Force close and reopen. Swipe Snapchat out of recent apps and relaunch.
  • Clear Snapchat cache. Profile → Settings → Clear Cache. Preserves your memories.
  • Switch networks. Toggle Wi-Fi off to use mobile data.
  • Log out and back in. Refreshes your session. May require re-authentication.
  • Update the app. Snapchat retires old versions regularly.
  • Reinstall. Last resort — will log you out and clear some local memories.

Frequently asked questions

Is Snapchat down right now?

Run the live check above. You'll see global status plus real-time community reports.

Why won't Snaps send?

Either Snapchat is down, your phone lost signal, or the app cache is corrupt. Try again after 10 minutes.

Why is Snap Map not loading?

Snap Map runs on a separate service. It can be down while the rest of Snapchat works.

Is Snapchat+ down?

Snapchat+ shares core backend. Partial outages sometimes only affect Snapchat+ features.

Why am I locked out?

Usually an account action — third-party tweak detection or spam flag. Not a Snapchat outage.
