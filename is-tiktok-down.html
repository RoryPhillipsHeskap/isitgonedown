Why is TikTok not working?

1. A TikTok-side outage

TikTok has had multiple high-profile outages in the past. When it happens, millions of users see the same "no internet" or "empty feed" errors at once, and community reports spike rapidly.

2. Country-level restriction

TikTok is banned in India and restricted in several other countries. If TikTok works over mobile data but not on Wi-Fi, your ISP or network may be blocking it.

3. App cache issue

TikTok's feed is aggressively cached. A corrupted cache can cause the "no videos" empty-state even when the app and server are fine. Clearing cache almost always fixes this.

4. Account shadow-ban

If TikTok is up for everyone but your videos get zero views, your account may be shadow-banned — the app works but your content is hidden from the algorithm. Check via a friend's account.

5. Upload infrastructure

TikTok's upload/processing service is separate from playback. It's common for you to browse videos fine but fail to upload. This is a partial outage, not a full one.

6. VPN conflict

TikTok detects and blocks many VPN IPs. If you're on a VPN and TikTok suddenly stops working, try disabling the VPN — it may be flagged.

What to do if TikTok is only down for you

  • Force close the TikTok app. Swipe it away and relaunch.
  • Try TikTok Web. If tiktok.com works in a browser but the app fails, it's an app issue.
  • Clear app cache. Profile → Settings and Privacy → Cache & Cellular Data → Clear Cache.
  • Switch networks. Toggle off Wi-Fi and use mobile data. If it works, your Wi-Fi is blocked.
  • Turn off your VPN. TikTok actively blocks many VPN IPs.
  • Update the app. Older versions can silently stop working.

Frequently asked questions

Is TikTok down right now?

Is TikTok banned in my country?

TikTok is banned in India and restricted in a handful of other countries. If it fails only on your home network, check whether your country or ISP has blocked it.

Why does the For You feed say 'no videos'?

Usually a cache issue. Clear the TikTok app cache or reinstall. Sometimes it's a backend issue with the recommendation service.

Is TikTok Shop down?

Shop runs on separate checkout infrastructure and can fail independently of video playback.

Why can't I upload?

Usually a TikTok-side processing issue, not a full outage. Check again in 15–30 minutes.
